Question

Is ATTAX Indonesia an official tax consultant?

Answer

Yes, we are an officially registered tax consultant in the Directorate General of Taxation, Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Indonesia, holding a Practice License Class C (the highest level in tax consulting in Indonesia). We are capable of handling individuals, domestic/local companies, and/or foreign capital companies.

Question

Is the price worth the value you receive?

Answer

We offer high-quality services at a fair price, ensuring you get great value for your money.

Question

How is ATTAX Indonesia different from others?

Answer

We are not just calculators, we want to educate and give you more knowledge. We are not only bookkeepers, but we also care about the development of your business and financial condition. We are not just ordinary consultants, we want to make sure your company gets a positive benefit from us.
